About Us


At Blood Reserve Center, we are committed to saving lives by ensuring a safe and adequate supply of blood for those in need. Our mission is to provide high-quality blood products and services to our community and beyond. We are a leading blood reserve center, serving hospitals and medical centers throughout the region.


Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced testing methods ensure the safety and effectiveness of every blood product we collect and distribute. Our team of highly skilled professionals includes experienced phlebotomists, technicians, and medical experts who work tirelessly to collect and process blood donations. We are passionate about our work and take pride in making a difference in the lives of those who need our help.


Whether you are a blood donor or a recipient, we are here for you. Our goal is to create a comfortable and welcoming environment for all who visit our center. We strive to make the donation process as easy and stress-free as possible, while also ensuring that every donation is used effectively to help those in need. Join us in our mission to save lives through blood donation.

Blood Request Process

  • A request for blood is received from a patient or hospital.
  • The staff checks the availability of blood in the blood bank.
  • If the blood is available, it is immediately reserved and arranged for delivery to the hospital or individual in need.
  • If the blood is not available, the staff sends an appeal to potential donors to donate blood.
  • Once a donor comes forward, the blood bank will examine the donor's blood to ensure it is safe for donation.
  • The patient or hospital receives the blood and the necessary treatment.

Blood Donors Requirements

  • Age: 18 years or older
  • Weight: At least 50 kilograms (110 pounds)
  • Health: Good health and free from infectious or chronic diseases
  • Lifestyle: No high-risk behaviors (unprotected sex, illegal drug use, etc.)
  • Travel: No recent travel to areas with a high risk of infectious diseases

How to Donate

  • Register by filling the form.
  • Provide correct information while filling the form.
  • Donate blood seven days a week at our main facility in Kathmandu (10:00 - 17:00)
  • If you are unable to visit our main facility in Kathmandu, our team will reach out to you when we organize blood donation in your area.
